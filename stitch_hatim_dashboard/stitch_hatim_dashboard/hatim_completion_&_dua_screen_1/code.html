Hatim Dua (Prayer)

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلِ القُرْآنَ لَنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا قَرِينًا، وَفِي القَبْرِ مُؤْنِسًا، وَفِي القِيَامَةِ شَفِيعًا

"O Allah, make the Quran for us in this world a companion, in the grave a consoler, and on the Day of Resurrection an intercessor."

Allahumma inni as'aluka khayra ma fi hadhal-Qur'an... O Allah, we ask You for the best of what is in this Quran. Make it a light for us and a guide.
